FAQ

Why doesn't the price show up in Trust Wallet?

Trust Wallet fetches the live price from websites like CMC and CoinGecko. If a token is not listed on these sites, then TW will not display a price.

How do you buy META SANTA FLOKI

$MSF can easily be bought on PancakeSwap. Please follow the steps below:

  • 1. Download Trust Wallet onto your mobile device
  • 2. Press Smart Chain BNB and then hit Buy
  • 3. Press on the DApps tab at the bottom and navigate to $MSF
  • 4. Hit Buy Now on the banner
  • 5. Press the settings icon at the top right and set slippage to 12-15%
  • 6. Enter the amount of $MSF you would like to purchase and hit Swap
  • 7. Confirm to receive your $MSF!
